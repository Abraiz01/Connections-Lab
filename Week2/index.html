Recollecting The Day

How often does one recollect and reflect on what happened during the day before they go to bed? Go ahead and try to remember or make a list as you power through your day. It could be something you ate for breakfast, a song you listened to, or a conversation you had with someone that sparked your curiosity or made you laugh. In the “Night” section, try to include something you look forward to for the next day, or a small goal you would like to achieve which could be anything from decorating your wall to calling a friend.
